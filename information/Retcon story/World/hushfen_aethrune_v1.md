# AETHRUNE — HUSHFEN (V1 — FULL LOCATION BIBLE)

## OVERVIEW

**Hushfen** is a drowned lowland region at the edge of the Shatterbelt, where memory, sound, and truth behave inconsistently.

It is not a settlement.
It is not a ruin in the traditional sense.
It is not controlled.

Hushfen is a **living archive of what was not properly recorded**.

Where Greywake attempts to define truth through systems, Hushfen exposes truth through:
- remnants
- echoes
- contradictions
- and things that were never meant to be remembered

---

# CORE IDENTITY

Hushfen exists to answer:

> “What remains when truth is not controlled?”

It represents:
- memory without structure
- warning without authority
- knowledge without interpretation

---

# FIRST IMPRESSION

When entering Hushfen, the player should feel:

- sound dampened unnaturally
- waterlogged terrain that shifts underfoot
- distant shapes that resemble structures but collapse on approach
- scattered remnants of habitation with no clear timeline
- markers that contradict each other

Nothing here feels fully present.

---

# GEOGRAPHY

Hushfen is composed of:

- shallow blackwater pools
- soft ground that gives way unpredictably
- broken pathways half-submerged
- reed clusters that obscure visibility
- collapsed timber frames
- old stone segments rising from mud

Movement is slow, uncertain, and constantly questioned.

---

# ENVIRONMENTAL BEHAVIOR

## SOUND

- footsteps are muted
- distant noises seem closer than they are
- spoken words carry unevenly
- silence can feel “heavy”

## LIGHT

- diffused through mist and moisture
- lantern light is absorbed rather than projected
- reflections behave inconsistently

## WATER

- not deep enough to drown easily
- deep enough to obscure
- carries debris, fragments, and sometimes objects that should not be there

---

# MICRO-LOCATIONS

## 1. THE SUNKEN PATH

A partially submerged road.

- visible stones beneath water
- path curves in ways that don’t match map expectations

Use:
- navigation puzzle
- route mistrust

---

## 2. THE LISTENING POSTS

Old wooden poles placed irregularly.

- some vibrate faintly
- some are broken

Use:
- signal interaction
- environmental storytelling

---

## 3. THE DROWNED CAMP

Remains of an expedition site.

- half-buried supplies
- abandoned notes
- signs of sudden departure

Use:
- lore discovery
- missing team quest

---

## 4. THE WHISPER REEDS

Dense plant clusters.

- produce sound when wind passes
- patterns sometimes resemble speech

Use:
- atmospheric pressure
- possible misdirection

---

## 5. THE MIRROR POOL

Still water with reflective distortion.

- reflections do not match reality exactly

Use:
- visual anomaly
- psychological tension

---

## 6. THE BROKEN BRIDGE

Collapsed crossing.

- multiple failed repair attempts
- no clear “correct” way across

Use:
- traversal challenge

---

## 7. THE NAME STONES

Scattered carved stones.

- names partially eroded
- some repeated

Use:
- memory theme reinforcement

---

## 8. THE LOW SIGNAL

Area where equipment fails.

- lantern flicker
- map inconsistencies

Use:
- system breakdown introduction

---

## 9. THE SILT FIELD

Open, flat terrain.

- appears safe
- conceals hazards beneath surface

Use:
- player caution test

---

## 10. THE STILL FRAME

Remains of a structure that appears intact from a distance.

- collapses upon interaction

Use:
- illusion of stability

---

# HISTORY

## PRE-COLLAPSE

Hushfen may have been:
- a managed drainage system
- a lowland support zone
- or part of a larger Meridian environmental control structure

## COLLAPSE

Systems failed.

Water spread.

Structures sank.

Records stopped being maintained.

## AFTER

Attempts to reclaim the area failed.

Maps became unreliable.

People stopped trusting formal documentation here.

---

# FACTION RELATIONSHIP

## GREYWAKE

- struggles to map Hushfen
- records inconsistent

## IRON HOLLOW

- avoids region unless necessary
- sees it as loss-heavy

## QUIET CHOIR

- highly interested
- views it as signal-rich

## RECLAIMERS

- cautious exploration
- high casualty rate

---

# GAMEPLAY SYSTEMS

## MEMORY DISTORTION

- markers may shift
- paths may loop

## SOUND MISREAD

- cues unreliable

## TRUST TESTING

- player must rely on judgment, not systems

---

# THEMATIC ROLE

Hushfen is:

- uncontrolled truth
- memory without authority
- warning without explanation

It contrasts:

- Greywake (controlled truth)
- Iron Hollow (lived survival)

---

# FINAL CONCEPT

Hushfen is where the world stops explaining itself.

It does not hide truth.

It simply refuses to organize it.

And that makes it one of the most dangerous places in Aethrune.
