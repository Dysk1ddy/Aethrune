# Aethrune Location Theme Map

This document maps the campaign's major locations to theme, pressure, enemy
ecology, companion use, and foreshadowing. It should be used with:

- `AETHRUNE_TRUTH_AND_BELIEF_MATRIX.md`
- `AETHRUNE_STORYBUILDING_PHASES_DRAFT.md`
- the existing location bibles in `information/Retcon story/World/`

## Purpose

Locations in Aethrune should feel like pressure zones within the route.

Each place should teach the player one specific thing about:

- control
- truth
- memory
- infrastructure
- adaptation
- or unresolved reality

The full campaign should therefore read as a thematic descent as well as a
geographical one.

## Campaign Escalation Spine

Use this as the stable high-level location arc:

1. `Emberway` teaches that passage is political.
2. `Iron Hollow` teaches that continuity depends on labor, discipline, and
   shared endurance.
3. `Greywake` teaches that maps and records can become authority.
4. `Hushfen / Pale Circuit` teaches that truth can exist without permission.
5. `Stonehollow` teaches that excavation turns curiosity into consequence.
6. `Glasswater / Broken Prospect / South Adit` teach that systems shape power,
   logistics, and people before anyone reaches the deepest ruin.
7. `Resonant Vaults` teach that the system learns the player.
8. `Blackglass Causeway / Meridian Forge` turn the act into a choice about what
   truth should be used for.
9. `Meridian Depths / Abyss` ask whether reality must be resolved at all.

## Location Family Rules

### Route spaces

Examples:
- Emberway
- Blackglass Causeway
- approach roads

They should emphasize:
- movement
- exposure
- fake authority
- route ownership
- who gets delayed and why

### Civic spaces

Examples:
- Iron Hollow
- claims hall
- shrines

They should emphasize:
- maintenance
- labor
- grief
- negotiation
- visible consequence

### Survey and certification spaces

Examples:
- Greywake
- survey camps

They should emphasize:
- procedure
- records
- amendments
- permitted access
- the emotional coldness of managed truth

### Excavation and infrastructure spaces

Examples:
- Stonehollow
- Glasswater Intake
- Broken Prospect

They should emphasize:
- practical systems
- rediscovery
- risk of reopening
- ethics of ownership

### Adaptive system spaces

Examples:
- Resonant Vaults
- Meridian Depths

They should emphasize:
- pattern learning
- anticipation
- geometry that answers behavior
- intelligence without intention

### Rupture spaces

Examples:
- Hushfen at its worst
- Abyss slips

They should emphasize:
- contradiction
- incomplete memory
- failed resolution
- the player losing the comfort of reliable interpretation

## Writing Rule

When revising or writing scenes, always ask:

What truth does this location pressure, and what human cost makes that pressure
real?

If a location cannot answer that question clearly, it needs another pass before
full prose production.
