# AETHRUNE — ENEMY TYPE CATEGORIES (V1)

## PURPOSE
Defines enemy categories in Aethrune with:
- gameplay role
- behavioral patterns
- lore grounding
- design usage

---

# CORE PRINCIPLE
Enemies are categorized by function and origin, not species.

---

# 1. HUMAN FACTIONS

## Route Controllers (Ashen Brand)
- Role: Territory control
- Behavior: coordinated ambushes
- Examples: Cutter, Marksman

## Quiet Choir Agents
- Role: Control / disruption
- Behavior: silence-based attacks
- Examples: Listener, Adept

## Reclaimers
- Role: Defensive units
- Behavior: formation-based combat

---

# 2. RUIN-ADAPTED BEINGS

## Cinderkin
- Role: swarm
- Behavior: traps + group attacks

## Tunnel Scavengers
- Role: ambush predator
- Behavior: blind tracking

## Hollow Giants
- Role: brute
- Behavior: slow heavy attacks

---

# 3. SYSTEM ENTITIES

## Meridian Constructs
- Role: guardians
- Behavior: patrol + target priority

## Observation Nodes
- Role: adaptive threat
- Example: Cistern Eye

## Resonance Forms
- Role: unstable entity
- Behavior: unpredictable

---

# 4. ENVIRONMENTAL PREDATORS

## Ashfang Beasts
- Role: pack hunters

## Carrion Stalkers
- Role: stealth predator

## Bloodmote Swarms
- Role: swarm pressure

---

# 5. CORRUPTED / SIGNAL-TOUCHED

## Starblighted
- Role: unstable humanoid

## Resonance Leeches
- Role: drain enemy

## Corrosive Mass
- Role: hazard

---

# DESIGN RULES
Each enemy defines:
- Role
- Behavior
- Origin
- Threat Type

---

# PROGRESSION

Early: factions + beasts  
Mid: ruin-adapted  
Late: system entities
